Our Cruise Ports

Welcome to our guide on cruising to Jamaica! Jamaica is a Caribbean gem known for its vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and warm hospitality. As you plan your cruise itinerary, you'll encounter several ports in Jamaica, each offering its own unique attractions and experiences. From the historic charm of Falmouth to the bustling energy of Montego Bay and the laid-back vibe of Ocho Rios, there's something for every traveler to enjoy.

Our Ports are located at

  1. Port of Montego Bay
    (Mantica Way, Montego Bay)
  2. Historic Port of Falmouth
    (2 King Street, P.O. Box 156, Falmouth, Trelawny, Jamaica W.I.)
  3. Port of Ocho Rios
    (Turtle Beach Road, Ocho Rios)
  4. Ken Wright Pier and the adjoining Errol Flynn Marina in Port Antonio, Portland (PO Box 188, Ken Wright Drive, Port Antonio, Portland)
  5. Historic Naval Dockyard in Port Royal
    (Port Royal, Kingston 1)
